MEETi is an app that keeps a record of everyone you meet by saving their name and the place you both met and significant information. With the use of your phone speaker, it is triggered with a specific keyword when every you meet someone new. In the future you don't need to stress your self by remembering anyone name or anything about them, this app will do that for you.
